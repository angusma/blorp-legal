# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

The short version: Blorp collects no personal data. This page exists to
say so properly.

## What we collect

Nothing. Blorp has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no trackers, and the
developer operates no servers that receive anything from your device.

## Where your progress lives

Your learning progress (XP, streak, mistakes, level estimates, road
position, settings) is stored only on your device. It is never uploaded.
Deleting the app deletes it. If you use device backups (e.g. iCloud), your
device may include this data in its backup under your own account — that
is between you and your backup provider.

## Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google
(Google Play). The developer receives no payment details and no identity
information from these transactions — only anonymous confirmation inside
the app that a purchase is active. Apple's and Google's own privacy
policies govern their processing.

## Speech (text-to-speech)

Exercise audio is generated by your device's built-in text-to-speech
voices. No audio or text is sent to the developer.

## Children

Blorp does not knowingly collect data from anyone — children included —
because it collects no data at all.

## Changes

If a future feature ever changes any of the above (for example optional
cloud sync), this policy will be updated first and the change made
obvious in the app.
